MALABAR BIRIYANI

At Awaafi Restaurant , we bring you a taste of Kerala’s grandest culinary celebration —the legendary Malabar Wedding Biryani . In the coastal kitchens of Malabar, biryani isn’t just food—it’s an art form . Our chefs have crafted this masterpiece by cooking it in a layered handi (clay pot) and slow-steamed (Dum style) for rich, aromatic perfection with the touch of mappila wedding feast.

ARABIC MANDI

At Awaafi, we serve Mandi with a story—a delicious fusion born when Keralites in the GCC blended Yemeni traditions with our spices. Returning home, they perfected it: slow-cooked Arabic-style meat meets bold Malabar masalas , creating the iconic Kerala Mandi you crave today. One dish, two cultures, endless flavor.
